Share Food

Mumbai Roti Bank is a non-profit, food rescue organisation initiated by Roti Foundation Mumbai that bridges the gap between hunger and excess food. Started in December 2017 under the able mentorship of former Director General of Police, Maharashtra, Mr. D. Sivanandhan, we aim to eliminate hunger, malnutrition and food wastage, many meals at a time.
